Topic 2:  Fine arts & its classification

        The overall operating scope of this organization comes under single umbrella called the "Fine arts" and this is what you need to understand in depth. A thorough description of its classification, sub-classfiation, styles, genres and examples are given in this topic.
Fine arts :   Any form of art with perfection involving sounds, colours, body movements... is called as Fine arts. The Practice of fine art is one of the ultimate forms involving mind, body & soul. It is the finest mode for concentration, memory development and spirituality which channelize for better living.

Fine arts is broadly classified in to following sections:

A. Vocal                               :   Human voice is used to render song.
B. Lead Instruments          :   Instruments that are used to play song (melody line).
C. Rhythm Instruments    :   Instruments that provide rhythm to song.
D. Dance                             :   An art form wherein feelings are expressed through body movements & facial expressions in-sync with music.
E. Visual Arts                      :   An art form used to express feelings through shades and colors. The need for eyes is obvious.

Each of the above sections except Visual arts are further divided into various styles such as Hindustani (North Indian), Carnatic (South Indian) & Western (Foreign). They are further divided based on different genres.
