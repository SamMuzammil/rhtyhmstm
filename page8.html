        The term inventory means the materials that we sell. Generally when any new student joins a course two items need to be procured. One will be the course material such as books and the other instruments - accessories (as per the course demand). There are courses which demand the mandatory purchase of instrument. Rhythms being a licensed dealer procures and sells the same as per the needs.
