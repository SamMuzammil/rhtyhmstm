15.13  PPT of all Types
        This will be the first sheet that will be available in the attendance book as well as the student status report. This acronym sheet carries a description of each and every event, opportunities, exams etc., a student would undergo in his entire journey with rhythms. Acronym details are mentioned next to the name of every student and would be updated time to time. Any new non-teaching staff/teacher can look at the acronym mentioned next to the student name and make out about his overall presence in the organization.



        This book is printed after roll-on on the first day of each month. It contains details of each and every active student, their contact numbers, grades, attendance etc. The front office admin will mark the student attendance and update it later on to the software. This book will also be used to call & update reports of students. There will be a circle marked for those who have paid the fees so that based on which one can call up and remind them about their pending fees. Any management team can get in the class to mark the attendance and will be able to remind the student on spot to pay the Fees.

        This particular stream being a service industry, lots of people are working in different profiles. Hence a clear performance report is maintained about the staff to understand who is really delivering the best and who is just backing. This report will be prepared by the HR manager at the end of the month and at the beginning of the next month, a meeting will be conducted discussing pros and cons. Good performers will be recognized and appreciated, and those who are not up to the mark will be advised and given some time to improve or might face termination.

This gives the clear picture to the teachers about the overall status of a student including his attendance and payment details.
