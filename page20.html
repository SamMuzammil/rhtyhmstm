Topic 8:  Cultural Events
8.2  Prathibhankura
        Different type of cultural events are organized throughout the year, some events are as an opportunity to students while some events are from top artists, and legends.
        "Rising stars Talent show" is an initiative by the organization especially to promote budding talents. Here students get a unique opportunity to perform in the presence of Chairman, Guest artists and mature audience.
8.1  Annual Fest

        To provide exceptional stage experience and to gain artistic skills, Rhythms conduct Annual Fest titled "Flames" in exalted auditoriums with sophisticated sound and lighting arrangements, featuring elite performances from students.
  • Carnatic Vocal
  • English Band
8.3  Visual Fest
        To promote art students an exclusive event Visual Fest is organized for each branch (every six months once). Guest artist will be invited to judge the artworks and best 3 artworks in each category i.e., Below 14 years and above 14 years will be awarded. 

8.4  Legendary Concerts
            Concerts of legends such as Pandit Ram Narayan, Pandit Bhimsen Joshi, Ustad Bismillah Khan,
Dr. Balamurali Krishna, Pandit Jasraj, Pandit Hariprasad Chaurasia, Pandit Venkatesh Kumar,... have been held by Rhythms with great honour.
8.5  Hazari Prasad memorial programme
        Annually music events are held by eminent artists in memory of Late Sri K. S. Hazari Prasad - an ardent music lover, father of Dr. Vishwanath Prasad. Proceeds of these events goes to charitable activities, specially to the visually impaired. Hundreds of individuals and NGOs are benefitted through these events.
8.6  Cultural tours
        Perfect platform to experience performing in celestial atmosphere and to get divine blessings. Tour to famous temples all over India are arranged frequently and elite students are given an opportunity to perform.
8.7  Faculty band recitals
        Faculty at Rhythms, enjoy the privilege of joining together as an amalgamation to form a band and get their performing thrust to be fulfilled through "Faculty band recitals".

8.8  Friday Bash
        Individuals, leading bands and groups get to perform in selective setup and in presence of mature audience in an open house organized on every Friday. Best performers are bestowed with additional opportunities from the organization.

8.9  Platform for budding artist
        Young as well as deserving artists are handpicked by the Organization and provided with an opportunity to perform at prestigious auditorium. This becomes a milestone and a platform to their blooming career.
