Topic 6:  Opportunities
6.2  Students band
        In the previous topics we discussed about the facilities which organization has provided as part of curriculum. The organization also provides golden opportunities for its serious learners to build and sustain their career in Fine arts industry. It could be an album project / scholarship / student exchange program, etc.,
        Exclusive Music albums, cover version and own compositions are made by students under the guidance of senior artists.
        This initiative brings discipline, co-ordination, multi-tasking, teamwork and creativity. Being in a band gives students a sense of pride and accomplishment.
6.4  Scholarship
6.3  High Flyer
       Serious and good learners in all grades in all courses are provided with an opportunity to get selected as High Flyers. High flyers enjoy following benefits:

  • Special training sessions from Chairman and guest artists
  • Discount on Projects of Rhythms like album, shows, events, etc.,
  • Opportunity to be a part of Rhythms students band
  • Fee reduction for higher grade learning based on certain parameters.

        To make fine arts reachable to underprivileged and visually impaired, the college provides scholarship through the Trust "Hazari Prasad Foundation" (a unit of Rhythms) and in association with Corporates, philanthropists.

6.5  Student Exchange Programs 
        We are closely associated with Industry stalwarts, connoisseurs across the world & with National - International boards. Dedicated students are handpicked for this golden opportunity who get global recognition & exposure. 
6.6  Youtube Elite
        Regularly shows, events and music programs are conducted to promote artistic skills in students. Best performances of these are selected and specially trained to appear in the channel Rhythms Elite which gets recognized in music fraternity worldwide.
