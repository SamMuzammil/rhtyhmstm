UPS operations
  1. The UPS connection provided is to work for the cameras and 7 tube lights.
  2. Whenever power goes, the UPS will switch on automatically, the computer should not shut down. If it shuts down or restarts, then call up the admin manager and inform the office.
  3. The switch behind the UPS should be in NC mode and the other in UPS mode.
  4. Understand the signs on the UPS.
  5. Sometimes, both KEB &UPS won't be working. The reason, battery might be used up or the fuse will be blown up. It will take 30 seconds to change the fuse & always keep a stock of it. First, switch off the main switch and the switch in the UPS then remove the fuse holder, throwaway the old fuse and put the new fuse. Then switch on. Sometimes, if there is a problem of UPS and KEB current is also proper, remove the plug which is put behind the UPS and put it in the plug-in the wall. By removing which is present there, then the power will be in KEB mode the person from U P service can come and pick up the UPS for repair.
