Topic 10:  Fees Structure

        This topic gives entire information of financial transactions that shall happen in the organization. You need to know what all kind of fee is collected like regular fee, admission fee, annual day fee, balance fee, etc., Besides this, every grade has various fees slab. All these have been clearly described in this topic along with related charts.

10.2  General Instructions :
  • One time Admission / Registration fees is Rs. 800 /-
  • Admission fees and monthly fees are not refundable / transferable.
  • Monthly fee and admission fee has to be paid in advance.
  • Monthly Fees is collected within 10th of every month.
  • If students are taking admission somewhere in the middle of the month i.e., between 12th and 25th, if possible charge full fees then calculate and give extra classes. 

  • If a student is taking admission between 25th and 30th, ask them to start the class from 1st of next month and just collect the admission fees.

  • 30% of the fees shall be collected despite complete absence of the student.
