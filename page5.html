Topic 1:  About Rhythms

1.1  Introduction
Welcome to the world of Fine arts - A universal language
The divine gift that never considers age, gender, religion or the monetary aspect
        Rhythms - a landmark premier Institution was established with a motto of creating awareness and promotion of fine arts in the society especially amongst the youth, to understand, appreciate and follow pure art forms.


The very name "Rhythms" conjures the musical movement with time. Established in 1996, Rhythms has created revolutionary highs in the sphere of fine arts, educating the masses by organizing concerts, lecture demonstrations, competitions, charity shows and social activities along with remarkable professional training.


We offer part time, full time and online courses related to 28 disciplines in Vocal, Instrumental, Dance and Visual arts in Indian and Western styles, providing "University Certified" programs with a credit system. Presently the organization cater more than 4,500 plus active students by around 125 esteemed faculty at our 7 branches prominently located across Bangalore for over two decades.


The devoted and disciplined voyage has made us the topmost college for Fine arts education not only in Karnataka but in entire India. Rhythms is closely associated with Industry stalwarts, connoisseurs across the world and with National - International exam boards making its presence felt globally.
