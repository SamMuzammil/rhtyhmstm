There are totally 18 topics and all are chain-linked and set in an orderly manner.


The first topic is "About us" that speaks about how the organization came into being, people behind it, its success stories and where you fall into the hierarchy of the staff.


The second topic is "Fine arts and its classification" which is the mainstream of our service. This topic will open your thought about the overall possibilities available to learn in the field of fine arts.


The third topic "Programs offered" speaks about the different type of programs that we offer, associated exam boards and certification.


Generally, courses have specific needs & demand for books and instruments. This is covered in the fourth topic "Inventory Management".


Every learner will be interested to know what all the facilities that the organization offers. This appeals more to students - parents and attracts them for joining Rhythms. This is covered in topic five.


"Opportunities" this sixth topic speaks about what extra a student can get by good learning. These are provisions that students can grab from the organization. Opportunities appeals more to the students - parents and becomes a strong base for them to prefer Rhythms.


Performances are the most significant and colourful aspect of learning. We do academic and cultural events. These two are covered respectively under the topics seventh and eighth.


The topic ninth provides details about all the events planned to be held in the calendar year. Around 30 to 40 types of events take place in a year. This calendar helps us to prepare beforehand and execute the programmes successfully.


Next comes topic tenth  wherein charges for the various services we provide are mentioned.


Being a diversified field, finalizing the course to pursue will be extremely challenging. The key reason for any learner to discontinue abruptly is because of not making the proper selection. Hence counselling effectively during admissions plays a very important role and the same is detailed in topic eleven.


With thousands of students and hundreds of staffs, keeping "A to Z" records of them through books is practically impossible. Hence, an advanced software "CMIS" is existing that holds each database to the fullest needs. The usage of this software is mentioned in topic twelve.


This organization has approximately 40 to 50 non-teaching staff. Their job profiles have been suitably designed and described. Non -teaching staff would include managers, front office admins, office assistants and security guards. All these are described in topic thirteen.


In the fourteenth topic, we would discuss the different kind of information and promotions through which we reach people. It could be the website, flyer, notice board, social media channels, etc.,


The organization has diversified activities through which some contents evolve time to time which can't be positioned in any categories. Such are discussed in this topic number fifteen.


Here in topic sixteen, through the trust "Hazari Prasad Foundation", we introduce you to social service activities carried out by Rhythms.


The Seventeenth topic carries details of questions that would be asked during evaluation.


And finally, a "Voyage video" will be played in topic eighteen, which is generally 8 mins. video, giving you a complete and emotional insight into the organization's journey and where we stand today.
