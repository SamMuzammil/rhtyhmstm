The main aim of this manual is to thoroughly educate new joinee as they will be part of this leading institution which has a rich legacy and has completed 27 successful years. One can imagine the challenges faced and how the organization might have adopted time to time to cater the needs of thousands of learners in this creative stream, while retaining its rich traditional heritage.


Since this is a service industry, coordination between various departments is very important for the organization's smooth functioning. Every employee needs to replenish their experience in the current business world and learn vital skills to perform better in their work and have to decide upon more than whatever has been mentioned in this training manual through sensible judgment.


Through this training manual, we want you to learn in depth the organization's complete functionality and thereby feel comfortable in execution of your job, enhancing efficiency and productiveness. Trainees should show a desire to improve by seeking feedback and advice from trainers. This training manual is prepared for managers and front office admin.


We very well know that this is a different stream of job especially for the "non-teaching department" to have come across. During this training you may face some challenges as the topics are different and terminologies being new. The best way to understand all of this is to equate it to an academic education, and as if you were a parent, and what would you expect the school to offer back to your ward? If you look at it from this angle, the process of learning becomes easy.
